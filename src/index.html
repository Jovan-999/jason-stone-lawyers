YOUR ATTORNEYS

Upon hiring Jason Stone Injury Lawyers, a dedicated legal team with be assigned to work with you to ensure that all your post-injury needs are being met.

Your legal team is assigned based on the severity and complexity of your situation. Every client has a personal case manager who is responsible for managing the legal and administrative aspects of your case and addressing all your post-injury challenges, so you don’t have to. An associate attorney is assigned to each client, and it is responsible for evaluating the evidence and determining the best course to ensure our clients are getting fully paid for everything that was taken from them due to someone else’s negligence. Finally, the attorneys work collaboratively with Jason, the managing attorney, and the senior attorneys in the firm, to evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of the evidence and formulate strategies to ensure a successful resolution for the client.

After getting injured, before you speak to an insurance company on your own, Better Phone Stone. Contact us anytime. We’re available 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, via phone, text, email and video meetings to provide advice, guidance, support, and, most importantly, legal expertise.

HOW THE JASON STONE INJURY LAWYERS TEAM WORKS Collaboratively For You

How Team Works Collaboratively For You

Jason Stone

Jason finds tremendous joy in regularly meeting with the associate attorneys and case managers assigned to a client’s injury case. They spend time evaluating the facts of the situation, examining the evidence, analyzing the strengths and weaknesses, and developing a well-reasoned plan of attack to ensure their clients gets paid for everything that was taken from them through no fault of their own.

Senior Attorney

In addition to regularly meeting with the associate attorneys to evaluate the facts of the loss and set strategy to ensure the associate attorney can achieve the most just outcome, the senior attorneys ensure that the associate attorneys live up to our core values when addressing the needs and wants of our clients.

Associate Attorney

The day-to-day handling of the various legal and strategic aspects of our clients’ injury cases are handled by the associate attorney. Whether it is negotiating a settlement, taking a deposition, meeting with clients, or ensuring we have all the evidence needed to effectively prove our clients’ damages, the associate attorney is the one that creates momentum and prevents the insurance companies from taking advantage of injured people.

Case Menager

The case manager is the manager of your legal team. Through the daily administration of your injury case, the case manager is inside of your file at least every two weeks. The case manager is responsible for making the process as easy as it can be for the injured person by documenting their injury related challenges in our case management system, helping schedule necessary medical care, obtaining evidence from various medical providers and state agencies, ensuring medical bills are paid and drafting various legal documents for attorney review.
